Who am I?

I am a recent graduate from the City College of New York where I double majored in History and Philosophy of science and technology and, and Computer Science. Born in Lahore, Pakistan and raised in New York City, I am excited to bring my knowledge of integration and institutional knowledge I've gained learning from meeting new people and experiences. Academically, my research interests lie in the interaction of human-computer theory, political theory, and South Asia Studies. I enjoy working with computer program technology, therefore, like to build websites, like this one, and enjoy analyzing data for either quantitative and qualitative inquiry. Furthermore, I am also working on a language learning website which allows users to learn Urdu, Persian, and Arabic interactively. Routinely, I write about my research, experiences, and interests - which you can find here.

Life outside of work

When I am not researching or programming, you can find me either playing cricket over the weekends, cooking, or biking. Currently, I am learning photography and hope to travel different parts of the world, utilizing my pixel and language skills. Also I try being funny, but it's mostly sarcasm.
